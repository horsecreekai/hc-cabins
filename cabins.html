Cardinal Cabin

Cabin

Comfortable and practical, Cardinal Cabin is a great choice for small groups or families looking for a clean, convenient stay near the trails. Located at 6069 Horse Creek Road in Spurlockville, WV, this cabin sits right next door to Creekside Cabin.

Check Availability

Description

Inside, you’ll find one bathroom, two queen beds and a twin loft bed, offering flexible sleeping arrangements. The cabin includes a kitchenette with an inductive cooktop, microwave, crockpot, and Keurig, along with linens and towels provided for a hassle-free stay. Outside, there’s plenty of room for trucks and trailers, making it an ideal basecamp for trail riders.

Policies/Rules

We welcome pets! All rentals are pet-friendly as long as pets are crated when left unattended. For the comfort of all guests, smoking is not allowed inside any unit. Weekend stays (Friday and Saturday) require a 2-night minimum, while single-night bookings are available on weekdays. Cancellations made 48 hours or more prior to check-in are eligible for a full refund.
