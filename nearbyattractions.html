Adventure, Family Fun, and Day Trips

Discover the best nearby attractions around Horse Creek Cabins.

From trail riding and water fun to quiet lake time and an easy Charleston day trip, there is plenty to explore during your stay.

Main Attraction

Hatfield-McCoy Trails — Ivy Branch

Ivy Branch is the signature attraction for many guests staying at Horse Creek Cabins. It is a great option for riders looking for off-road adventure close to the property.

This trail system is known for ATV, UTV, dirt bike, and full-sized off-road vehicle use, making it one of the strongest reasons many visitors choose to stay in the area.

Why Guests Love It

  • Also suitable for full-sized off-road vehicles
  • Easy anchor activity for a weekend trip
